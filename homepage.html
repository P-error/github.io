How to play?

Find the monster and click on it! That's literally all you need to know.
Some of the monsters can hide or become invisible. But your cursor may well expose them.
Remember that none of them wants to be caught - so they will try to escape from you.
Each captured zerg will bring you points. You can find out your score, as well as the number of non-captured monsters at the top of the site
And if you want to find out which monsters have already been caught by you, then open your bestiary
